## My Honest Recommendation

### Use FREE Email If:
- You have <100,000 products (99.9% of businesses)
- You want 100% free notifications
- You check email regularly
- Budget is important

### Use Gmail If:
- Personal business or small business testing
- Want simplest setup

- You're okay paying after 1,000 free messages
